Lumine Atelier is a creative art and craft studio offering inspiring workshops in painting, crochet, air-dry clay crafting, and miniature cake decorating.

Vision

To become a leading creative studio that inspires individuals to express themselves confidently through art and craftsmanship

Mission

  • To provide high-quality and engaging art & craft workshops.
  • To create a supportive and inclusive creative community.
  • To encourage self-expression and skill development through hands-on learning.
  • To nurture creativity in a warm and inspiring environment.
